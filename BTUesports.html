Welcome to the official website of BTU Esports

BTU esports is a competitve PUBG Mobile team of Bangladesh. It represents a wholesome of talented youngsters who are dedicated and ready to show their gaming skills to the esports industry. We are here to showcase the talented players around the country and want to make a quality team for the international stage."Play for pride" is the motto of BTU Esports.From PMCO spring split 2021, the journey of BTU Esports started. Now we have two stable lineups. The names are "BLxBTU Esports" & "BTUxYOUNGBLOOD".

About our Lineups

1.BLxBTU Esports

BLxBTU Esports is the 1st lineup of BTU Esports. It is a sponsored lineup which is sponsored by Brain Lag, a big and ancient PUBG Mobile clan from Bangladesh.The clan leader of Brain Lag, Faisel ahmed is the one who saw the potential of our players and has been guiding us from the first day of his presence in the team. This team have six players. The IGL(Ingame Leader) BTUesSENPAInub is leading the team from the very beginning. It is a well organised team to play official and unofficial tournaments of National & International stages.

2.BTUxYOUNGBLOOD

BTUxYOUNGBLOOD is the 2nd lineup of BTU Esports. This lineup is defined by the name YOUNGBLOOD. This lineup is full of young talents. We assume it as the future of BTU Esports.This lineup have 5 very young and crazy players. The IGL(Ingame Leader) BTUesLxrd is the brain of Youngblood. BTUesSENPAInub(The IGL of BLxBTU Esports) is the advisor and coach of the young team. This team has the potential to outrage enemies from the battleground. They are grinding and practicing in national unofficial tournaments and scrims. Recently they have got their first achievement as they placed 4th in the Zero2 weekly scrims.
